# Code Of Conduct

GitPulse should be a practical and respectful project for contributors and
users.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, constructive, and specific.
- Focus critique on code, design, behavior, and evidence.
- Respect different experience levels and operating systems.
- Keep issues and pull requests on topic.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic disruption after maintainers ask for focus.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block users; or
take other reasonable steps to keep the project productive.

If you see behavior that violates this code, contact the maintainers through the
repository's moderation or security channels.
